Jia Ern Loy

home

After 10 years in academia, I finally decided it was time to say goodbye and pursue other things. I currently work as a data visualisation and front-end engineer at Agora Energiewende. I spend most of my time building web apps that let you explore and visualise climate data, and give the occasional training on how to create cool visualisations.

Previously, I did post-doc stints in Vera Demberg's lab at Saarland University and the Centre for Language Evolution at the University of Edinburgh, where I worked on topics such as individual differences and the pragmatics of communication. You can find out more about my past research here.
